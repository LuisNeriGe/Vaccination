Preparations Before Vaccine

the success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's inmune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:

    Avoid alcoholic beverages

Alcohol is a toxic and psychoactive substance with
dependence producing properties. In many of today's
societies, alcoholic beverages are a routine part of
the social landscape for many in the population.

    Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on inmune work.
Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol
production and oxidative stress in the body.

    Eat healthy food

Eat healthy means following a healthy eating pattern
that includes a variety of nutritious foods and drinks.
It also means getting the number of calories that's right
for you.

    Get enough sleep

Most adults need 7 or more hours of good-quality
sleep on a regular schedule each night.

    Exercise or physical activity

Exercise delivers oxigen and nutrients to your
tissues and helps your cardiovascular system
work more efficiently.
